# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 18, 2025**  
**Last Updated: December 18, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to our web-based booking system ("the Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

We are the data controller responsible for your personal data. Our Service is designed with Privacy by Design principles, collecting only the minimum data necessary for functionality.

If you have questions, contact us at: kirjaamo@centria.fi

## 1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect limited personal data directly from you:

- **Account Information**: Email address (used as username) and password (securely hashed).
- **Booking Information**: Selected resource and time slot for hourly bookings.
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, and device information (via essential session cookies only).

We do not collect special categories of personal data (e.g., health, ethnicity) or data from children under 15.

## 2. How We Collect Personal Data

- **Directly from you**: During registration, login, and booking.
- **Automatically**: Through essential cookies (e.g., csrf_token for security) when you interact with the Service.

## 3. Purposes and Legal Bases for Processing

We process your data for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Legal Basis (GDPR Article 6) |
| ------- | ---------------------------- |
| Account creation, authentication, and session management | Contract fulfillment (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Processing and managing bookings | Contract fulfillment (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Enforcing age restriction (minimum 15 years) | Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) |
| Security and fraud prevention (e.g., CSRF protection) | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| System administration and improvement | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |

We do not use your data for marketing or profiling.

## 4. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except:

- **Service Providers**: Hosting and technical providers under strict Data Processing Agreements.
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or authorities.

All data is stored within the European Economic Area (EEA). No international transfers occur.

## 5. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active.
- Booking data: Retained as needed for operational purposes.
- Upon account deletion: All personal data is permanently erased within 30 days.

## 6. Your GDPR Rights

As an EU resident, you have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure** ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your data.
- **Restriction**: Limit processing in certain cases.
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format.
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Where applicable (though we primarily rely on contract/legal bases).

To exercise rights, contact kirjaamo@centria.fi. We respond within one month.

You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

## 7. Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational measures:

- Strong password hashing.
- CSRF tokens for form protection.
- Secure session management.
- Regular security assessments (e.g., ZAP scans showing no critical vulnerabilities).

## 8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use only strictly necessary session cookies (e.g., csrf_token). No analytics, marketing, or tracking cookies. See our separate Cookie Policy for details.

## 9. Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a breach posing risk to your rights, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and inform you without undue delay.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 15. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via the Service or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
